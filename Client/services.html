Rentals

Fight the high price of gas and rent locally!

You may rent the instrument of your choice on a monthly basis at a low monthly rental payment which includes FREE insurance against fire, theft, and loss.

You may rent for as long as you desire before having to make a purchase decision.

Statements will be sent for monthly or quarterly rental payments. Rental payments must be made monthly in advance.

You may return the instrument to our store at anytime or if you do not with to continue in the music program. Return of the instrument - provided the rental payments are current - terminates the agreement, and you are under no further obligation.

Instruments Rental Prices:

Flutes, Clarinets, Trumpets, Trombones --- starting at $20

Alto Saxephones, Oboes, Cellos, French Horns --- starting at $30

Tenor Saxephones, Baritones --- starting at $35

Violins, Violas, Bell Kits --- starting at $18

*All prices listed include tax

Repairs

Free repair service will be performed on the instrument during the duration of the rental period. This service covers normal wear and use, but it does not cover willful damage due to careless handling. Please call the store ahead of time if you need a loner instrument to use.

If your instrument ever needs repair, Hardt's Horn Hospital will make everyone feel better! When your horn is admitted for repairs, we will borrow you an instrument FREE OF CHARGE until your instrument is discharged!
